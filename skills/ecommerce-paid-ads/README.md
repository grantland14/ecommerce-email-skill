# eCommerce Paid Ads Skill for Claude

An expert paid advertising strategist built as a Claude skill. Plan and optimize Meta, Google, and TikTok ad campaigns that drive profitable customer acquisition for DTC brands.

## What it does

This skill turns Claude into a performance marketing expert who understands DTC advertising inside and out. It covers:

- **Meta Ads** — Campaign structure, audience targeting, creative strategy, scaling, and optimization
- **Google Ads** — Search, Shopping, and Performance Max campaigns for eCommerce
- **TikTok Ads** — Creative-first strategy, Spark Ads, creator whitelisting
- **Ad creative** — Copy formulas, hook structures, UGC briefs, and creative testing frameworks
- **Campaign architecture** — Full-funnel structure from prospecting to retargeting to retention
- **Budget allocation** — How to split spend across platforms based on performance
- **Measurement** — ROAS targets, MER tracking, and platform attribution realities

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repo
2. Open [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and go to **Settings → Skills**
3. Upload the `SKILL.md` file from `skills/ecommerce-paid-ads/`
4. Start a new conversation — Claude will now advise on your ad campaigns like a media buyer

## Example prompts

- *"Build a Meta Ads campaign structure for my $60 AOV skincare brand. Budget: $5K/month"*
- *"Write 5 ad hooks for a Facebook carousel ad promoting our bestselling moisturizer"*
- *"My Meta ROAS dropped from 3.5x to 2.1x this week — help me diagnose"*
- *"Plan a Google Shopping campaign for our 50-SKU apparel brand"*
- *"Create a UGC creator brief for TikTok ads — we sell premium protein bars"*

## Who this is for

DTC founders running their own ads or managing an agency, who want Claude to give expert-level ad advice tailored to eCommerce — not generic PPC guidance.
