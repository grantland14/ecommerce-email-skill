# eCommerce Social Content Skill for Claude

A social media content strategist for DTC brands, built as a Claude skill. Plan content calendars, write platform-specific copy, develop UGC strategies, and create content that builds brand and drives traffic.

## What it does

This skill turns Claude into a social media expert who understands how DTC brands use social to drive awareness, engagement, and revenue. It covers:

- **6 content pillars** — Product, educational, social proof, brand story, community, and promotional content
- **Platform strategies** — Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, and Facebook with platform-specific tactics
- **Content calendars** — Weekly and monthly planning templates
- **Social copy formulas** — Captions, hooks, and CTAs that drive engagement
- **UGC and creator strategy** — Finding creators, writing briefs, managing content
- **Content ideas by scenario** — Product launches, seasonal moments, slow periods, and trend-jacking
- **Measurement** — Which metrics matter and what to ignore

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repo
2. Open [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and go to **Settings → Skills**
3. Upload the `SKILL.md` file from `skills/ecommerce-social-content/`
4. Start a new conversation — Claude will plan and write your social content

## Example prompts

- *"Create a 4-week content calendar for my DTC coffee brand on Instagram and TikTok"*
- *"Write 10 Instagram caption options for our new product launch"*
- *"Build a UGC creator brief for TikTok — we sell $45 candles"*
- *"What should I post this week? I have no product launches, just need to stay active"*
- *"Plan our social strategy for the holiday season — November through December"*

## Who this is for

DTC founders and small marketing teams who need a consistent social media presence but don't have a dedicated social media manager. Get strategic, brand-aware content planning instead of posting random product photos.
